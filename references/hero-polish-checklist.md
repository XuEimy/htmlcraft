# Hero Polish Checklist

Use this checklist during the polish phase for any homepage or landing page with a meaningful hero.

## Core Principle

Hero polish is not a shadow tweak.

It is the phase where the first screen becomes memorable, intentional, and structurally precise
without changing the approved archetype.

## What To Tighten

### 1. Headline Breaking

Check:

- line breaks feel intentional rather than accidental
- the line stack supports the hero archetype
- the longest line does not kill the rhythm
- multilingual headline rhythm still feels composed

### 2. Copy Density

Check:

- the hero copy is not overstuffed
- the supporting paragraph is not too weak for the chosen archetype
- manifesto-style heroes stay concise
- proof-first heroes do not bury the headline

### 3. Anchor Strength

Check:

- the hero has one unmistakable dominant anchor
- secondary UI, chips, labels, badges, and stats do not compete with the anchor
- decorative elements are not louder than the intended focal point

### 4. CTA Tension

Check:

- the primary CTA has the right weight
- secondary CTA is visibly secondary
- CTA placement helps the archetype instead of dragging it back to a template

### 5. Hero Height And Breathing Room

Check:

- the hero is not accidentally too short
- the hero is not padded into emptiness without purpose
- the breathing room fits the archetype

### 6. Hero To Section 2 Flow

Check:

- the first scroll feels authored
- section 2 belongs to the same story
- the user does not feel like the page restarted after the hero

### 7. Archetype Purity

Check:

- split hero does not become generic SaaS chrome
- poster hero does not get overrun by UI furniture
- manifesto hero does not accumulate random media blocks
- proof-first hero does not become visually dead
- gallery hero still has hierarchy
- docked hero still feels interactive rather than cluttered

## Recommended Polish Output Shape

```md
## Hero Polish

| Category | Before | After | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Headline rhythm | ... | ... | ... |
| CTA tension | ... | ... | ... |
| Hero -> section 2 | ... | ... | ... |
```

Then summarize:

- what became stronger
- what stayed intentionally unchanged
- any remaining hero risks

## Final Rule

If the page still feels "like a good template" rather than "like this page," the hero probably
needs one more polish pass.
